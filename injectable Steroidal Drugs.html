DEXARON INJECTION 2mg

Compostion

Each ml contains:
Dexamethasone Sodium Phosphate eq to Dexamethasone Base 2mg Description:

Indications

Dexamethasone is a glucocorticosteroid with a strong antiflogistic, anti-allergic and gluconeogenetic action. Acetone anemia, allergies, arthritis, bursitis, shock, and tendovaginitis in calves, cats, cattle, dogs, goats, sheep and swine.

Contra indications:

Unless abortion or early parturition is required, administration of DEXARON during the last trimester of gestation is contra-indicated. Administration to animals with an impaired kidney or heart function. Osteoporosis.

Dosage:

For intramuscular or intravenous administration.

Cattle	:5 - 15 ml.
Calves, goats, sheep and swine: 1 - 2.5 ml.

Dogs	:0.25 - 1 ml.
Cats	:0.25 ml.

Withdrawal times:

For meat:3 days. For milk: 1 day. Warning: Keep out of reach of children.

Available Packing:

Vial of 50 ml & 100ml

Acetosol Injection

Compostion

Prednisolone Acetate (B.P.) 10 mg

Indications

ACETOSOL injection has pronounced Anti-Inflammatory, Anti-Allergic ACETOSOL injection is effective drug in Eczema, Dermatitis (non-specific) & all kinds of inflammations. ACETOSOL injection is indicated in ketosis (acetonemia), urticaria, stress, shock, arthritis, tendinitis & inflammatory joint lesions. For the treatment of inflammatory disorders e.g. Bursitis, Mucositis. Tendinitis, Conjunctivitis, tenosynovitis etc. Allergic states e.g. Eczema, Dermatitis etc. Asthmatic disorders of young animals.

DOSAGE & ADMINISTRATION

For Systemic injection is given deep into Muscle.

ANIMALS          DOSAGE
CATTLE           5-20 ml
CALVES           2.5-5 ml
DOGS & CATS:     1 - 3 ml
WITHDRAWAL TIME: 
Milk: 3 Days. 
Meat: 5 Days.

INSTRUCTIONS
