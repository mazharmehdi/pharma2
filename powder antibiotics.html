NEOJIN POWDER

Composition

Each gm contain:
Neomycin sulphate:………..300mg

Indications

NEOJIN POWDER is indicated for the prevention and treatment of bacterial enteritis in calves, sheep, goats, swine and poultry caused by bacteria susceptible to neomycin, such as E. coli, Salmonella and Campylobacter spp.

Contra indications

Hypersensitivity to neomycin. Administration to animals with a seriously impaired renal function.
Administration to animals with an active microbial digestion.Administration during gestation.
Administration to poultry producing eggs for human consumption.

Side effects

Neomycin’s typical toxic effects (nephrotoxicity, deafness, neuromuscular blockade) are generally not produced when it is administered orally. No additional side effects are to be expected when the prescribed dosage regimen is followed correctly.

Dosage 

For oral administration:
Calves, goats and sheep:
10 mg neomycin sulphate per kg body weight 
(equivalent to 30 mg/kg NEOJIN POWDER) for 3 -5 days.
Poultry and swine:
600 g per 2000 liter drinking water for 3 - 5 days.
Note: for pre-ruminant calves, lambs and kids only.

Available Packing

5gm, 10gm, 100gm, 250gm, 500gm, 1000gm.
