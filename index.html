About Us

Family History

Our family is originally related with the medicine business since 1934 .Late Sh.Hakim Waris Jawa started his work in lahore in 1934 by the name of Jawa Pansari store and served the humanity . Sh, Shahid Ali Jawa have observed the drastic changes in the field of medicine and he acquired a pharmaceutical company by the name of IRZA Pharma (pvt) Ltd. The company was restructured after the implimation of Drug Act 1976 in 1977 and is still working as one of the leading pharmaceutical company of Pakistan.In 1981 the intelligent and obedient. Ali Akbar Jawa, Amjad Ali Jawa andSajid Ali Jawa who are the nephews of Sh. Shahid Ali Jawa established a new unit Wilshire labs(pvt) Ltd. After the success of the organization,they felt to diversify the company in order to serve more to the society. A star from this galaxy, Sh.Ali Akbar Jawa started establishing a unit in 2003 for veternary pharmaceutical by the name of International Pharma Labs which was completed in August 2005.This company started serving the world under the supervision of highly qualified and experienced staff.
