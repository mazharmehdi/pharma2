I-FONE-C INJECTION

Compostion

EACH 100ml CONTAIN
CALCIUM GLUCONATE 26.6gm ,
BORIC ACID 5.4 gm,
MAGNESIUM CHLORIDE 2 gm,
MAGNESIUM HYPOPHOSPHITE 5.33gm,
CALCIUM – D-SACCHARATE 1 gm and DEXTROSE ANHYDROUS 20gm

Indications

The product is recommended for the treatment of hypocalcaemia in cattle before, during, and after birth, or during lactation, when complicated by magnesium and phosphorus deficiency.

Dosage: 

Administer by subcutaneous or slow intravenous injection, using aseptic technique.
The actual dose will depend upon the clinical condition of the animal, and the amount to use is left to the expertise of the Veterinary Surgeon who may direct accordingly.
As a guide it is suggested that a dose of 800 – 1,500 mg of calcium per 50 kg be used corresponding the following:
Cattle – up to 500 ml.
Withdrawal times:
Milk: NIL 
Meat: NIL

Warning

Administer with care particularly where concurrent toxaemia exists. The solution should be warmed to body temperature before use. Intravenous injections should be carried out slowly and stopped at once if untoward symptoms occur. When administered subcutaneously massage the injection • Avoid the introduction of contamination during use.

Available packing:

300ml and 450ml.

As a therapy in weakness and exhaustion, as a supplement to antibiotics in acute and severe infections like coli mastitis, septicemia, enteritis, pneumonia, in transportation diseases, ketosis, acetonaemia, as a febrifuge and anodyne and for stimulating appetite and movement in animals. JADE INJECTION

Compostion

Each ml contains:
Vitamin D3 40000IU
Vitamin A 80000 IU
Vitamin E 20.0mg

Indications

Prevention and therapy of hypo- and a vitaminoses. Vitamin deficiency after infectious and infestative diseases of cattle, calves, swine, nurslings, sheep, goats, dogs and cats treated with sulphonamide, antibiotics and with anthelmintics.

Dosage:

For subcutaneous administration. 

Cattle, horses:	
0.2 ml / 10 kg l. w.
Calves:	
0.4 ml/ 10 kg l. w.
Swine, nurslings, sheep, goats:
0.2 ml / 10 kg l. w.
Dogs:	0.8 ml / 10 kg l. w.
Cats:	0.1 ml /   1 kg l. w.

Administration:

Intramuscularly or subcutaneously – cattle, horse, calves, swine, nurslings, sheep, goats. Subcutaneously dogs, cats. The dose should be divided and injected at several sites. .

Storage:

Store below 25 °C, protect against light and frost. After opening store at 2-8 °C.

I-EVITS INJECTION

Compostion:

Each ml contains:
Vitamin E 75 mg
Sodium Selenite 0.6mg

Indications:

Vitamin E deficiencies (like encephalomalacia, muscular dystrophy, exudative diathesis, infertility problems) in calves, cattle, goats, sheep and swine. Prevention of iron-intoxication after administration of iron to piglets.

Side effects:

No undesirable effects are to be expected when the prescribed dosage regimen is followed. 

Dosage:
For intramuscular or subcutaneous administration: 
Calves, goats and sheep:
1.5-2 ml. per 10 kg. body weight, repeat after 2 - 3 weeks.
Swine:
.75-1 ml. per 10 kg. body weight, repeat after 2 - 3 weeks.

Withdrawal times:

None.

Available Packing:

10ml, 20ml, 50ml and 100ml.
